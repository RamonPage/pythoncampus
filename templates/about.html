About PythonCampus

What is it?

PythonCampus is a technological circus that visits universities integrating students to the industry through Python and Open Source software. This is the event that shows it's possible to transform passion about technology into a successful career.

It's a full day of talks, tutorials, coding dojos and open spaces, where everybody participates sharing experiences while making good friends.

How it works?

Community members join forces to setup a rich agenda focused on Open Source, Agile, Python and emerging technologies taking care of the logistics.

On the other front, the university provides the infrastructure needed to make a great event.

Together, we spread the word so all students get to know about this great opportunity.

How to participate?

Join the PythOnRio community mailing list or contact us so we can take PythonCampus to your university.

About us

We are entrepreneurs, professionals, students, professors and enthusiasts, passionate about technology and sharing a strong belief in Open Source principles.

We are working to turn our community into the world's biggest bazaar with view to the beach.
